RESTAURANTS

Auckland is filled with delightful, stunning restaurants that are perfect for your friends and family. Here are just a few for you to try out!

1. ORIGINE

A perfect pairing new restaurant, Origine combines classic French cuisine with modern Kiwi approach. Origine is a modern French restaurant with a relaxed New Zealand atmosphere, an intersection of classical French cuisine.

2. JANG TEO

Jang Teo is a authentic Korean cuisine restuarant that is located in Korea town in Wairau Valley in Auckland. It's a lovely place to eat if you're looking for simple, soothing comfort food but also want to try out some new dishes.

3. COCORO

Cocoro is a modern Japanese restaurant which is located in Ponsonby, Auckland. Cocoro offers small plates & set-course menus of a varity of fancy Japanese cuisine starting from fesh fish to lovely soothing udon.

5. TEN-HANA

Ten-Hana is a casual Japanese restuarant that specialises in Tempura bowls. It is located in Crowne Plaza Auckland. They serve a decent amount of food for the price you pay for which is also a bonus. Great for University students who have no idea for to go and don't want to spend massive amounts of many on food. The location of it is also perfect as it is very close to the Auckland CBD.

4. SU & ZHE

Su & Zhe is a authentic chinese restaurant which is located on Dominion Road, Mount Eden, Auckland. It serves amazing dencent portions of food for a reasonable price. Su & Zhe serves amazing genuine chinese food that is very close to what you would have in China. It's a lovely, comforting restuarant for which it is great to go with fammily and friends.

5. Mr Lobster Private Kitchen

Mr Lobster Private Kitchen is a modern twist on Chinese Yum Cha. It's a lovely place for a family gathering. It is located on Lincon Road, Henderson, Auckland. Great staff members who a friendly and accomodating.

DESSERTS

If you're ever on the streets of Auckland craving for something sweets you are in the perfect place. Auckland is filled with amazing dessert places that will satisfy your needs of something sweet.

1. MÖVENPICK

Mövenpick Ice Cream is a brand of ice cream of Swiss origin produced by Nestlé, and now is the new company called Froneri since 2016 in joint-venture with Nestlé and R&R Ice Cream.

2. BLUEBELLS

Bringing sweetness and joy to your celebrations! Our bakery is passionate about creating delicious, handcrafted cakes, cupcakes, and other baked goods that look as good as they taste.We believe that every occasion is special, and our mission is to make it even more memorable with our sweet treats.

3. KRISPY KREME

Krispy Kreme is best known for fresh, glazed, yeast-raised doughnuts, especially the "Krispy Kreme Original Glazed," its first and best-known doughnut. All of its doughnuts are made from a secret recipe that has been in the company since 1937.

4. DUNKIN' DOUGHNUTS

Dunkin' Donuts is recognized for their amazing selection of incredibly tasting donuts. Their website lists over 50 possible donut flavours, and they always have seasonal specials, so you can bet they'll have exactly what you're craving for.
